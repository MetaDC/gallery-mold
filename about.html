About Gallery Mold

Bridging innovative art practices with contemporary aesthetics

Our Story

A Vision for Contemporary Art

Gallery MOLD began just six months ago with a simple dream — to create a space in Vadodara where young and emerging artists could grow and share their voices. Vadodara has always been a city rich in art and culture, and we wanted to add a new chapter to that story. Our vision is to build a community where creativity thrives and young audiences can experience art that inspires, challenges, and connects.

Curating Excellence

Designed with an international outlook, Gallery MOLD brings a fresh, contemporary lens to art. It’s a space where modern ideas, experimental works, and interactive experiences come together. Founded by *Shilpa Parikh*, an art enthusiast who has explored galleries across the world, MOLD reflects her belief that Vadodara holds immense creative potential. Through this space, we hope to share that spirit with the world and help shape the city’s next era of art and culture.
